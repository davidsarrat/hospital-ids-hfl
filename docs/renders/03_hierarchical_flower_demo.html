03 - Hierarchical Flower Demo

This notebook runs the target three-layer topology:

  • Hospital SuperNodes train on local CIC-IDS2017 partitions.
  • Regional SuperLinks aggregate hospital updates into regional checkpoints.
  • RegionGateway SuperNodes expose only regional checkpoints to the global SuperLink.
  • The global SuperLink aggregates regional models into the global checkpoint.
